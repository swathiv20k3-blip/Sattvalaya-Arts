Meet Rajalakshmi Roshan

Rajalakshmi Roshan is a biotechnologist by profession and a Vainika, Bharatanatyam dancer, and yoga practitioner by passion. Her journey with Veena began at the age of 11 under the guidance of Adi Chitra Alangara One-Foot Veena Vidwan Guru Shri V. S. Rangarajan, whose teachings continue to inspire her every day.

She completed her Veena Arangetram (solo debut) in 2005 in Chennai, India, and went on to win second place at the 2006 Musical Talent Hunt Contest hosted by Anna FM. Over the years, she has performed in several Veena concerts around Chennai, sharing her love for this beautiful instrument with many.

Born and raised in Chennai, the cultural heart of India, Rajalakshmi grew up surrounded by music and dance. She deeply values her cultural roots and the blessings of her guru, who instilled in her both the passion and discipline that define her musical journey.
